# Tacit

**Express and protect your brand in the age of the agent.**

Tacit is the company building the standard for how brands express and protect their intent in the age of the agent.

We encode a brand's strategy, voice, and judgment into hierarchical files every agent in the stack can read — authored once, consumed everywhere. So a brand gets stronger the more agentic its marketing becomes, not weaker.

Brand is more valuable in the agentic age, not less. The brands that win are the ones whose judgment is encoded into the system itself. Tacit encodes tacit craft. That's what we do.
